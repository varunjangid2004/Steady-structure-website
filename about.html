Our Story

From Jaipur to
Pan-India

Steady Structure Forming was established with a single purpose — to manufacture solar panel mounting structures that last. Based out of Transport Nagar, Jaipur, we began as a small fabrication unit and have grown into one of Rajasthan's most recognised names in solar mounting solutions.

Over the past 6+ years, we have supplied structures to solar contractors, EPC companies, developers, institutions, and homeowners across India. Every structure that leaves our facility is fabricated in-house, quality-checked, and built to withstand decades of exposure to the elements.

Our product range covers the full spectrum — single pole ground mounts, rooftop structures, tin shade systems, and a complete line of hardware components. Whether it's a 1 kW home rooftop or a 500 kW industrial project, we have the capacity and the expertise to deliver on time.
